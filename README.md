# Walmart Sales Performance Dashboard

## Overview
[cite_start]This dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of **$6.74bn** in total sales for Walmart Neighborhood Market stores[cite: 9, 10]. [cite_start]The report spans from February 2010 to October 2012, tracking how external factors like economic indicators and weather conditions impact retail performance[cite: 5, 7].

---

The top section of the dashboard highlights the primary averages and totals across the monitored period:
* [cite_start]**Total Sales:** $6.74 Billion [cite: 9, 10]
* [cite_start]**Average Fuel Price:** $3.36 [cite: 11, 12]
* [cite_start]**Average CPI (Consumer Price Index):** $171.58 [cite: 13, 15]
* [cite_start]**Average Unemployment Rate:** 8.00% [cite: 14, 16]
* [cite_start]**Average Temperature:** 60.66°F [cite: 39, 40]

## Sales Trends & Influencing Factors

### 1. Temporal and Seasonal Trends
* [cite_start]**Holiday Impact:** Non-holiday weeks account for the vast majority of sales (92.5%), while holiday weeks contribute 7.5%[cite: 26, 29]. [cite_start]Notable sales peaks occur during February, September, November, and December[cite: 78, 81, 83, 84].
* [cite_start]**Annual Performance:** The dashboard tracks total sales and temperature fluctuations through 2011 and 2012, showing consistent seasonal patterns[cite: 41, 45, 46].

### 2. Economic Correlations
* **CPI & Unemployment:** Sales are analyzed against the Consumer Price Index and Unemployment rates. [cite_start]Currently, the "High" CPI level accounts for 50.57% of sales compared to 49.43% for "Low" CPI[cite: 108, 109].
* [cite_start]**Unemployment Levels:** "Low" unemployment levels correlate with 55.12% of total sales, while "High" unemployment levels account for 44.88%[cite: 86, 87].
* [cite_start]**Fuel Prices:** Sales are slightly higher when fuel prices are categorized as "Low" (52.23%) compared to "High" (47.77%)[cite: 99, 105, 106].

### 3. Weather Conditions
Sales are segmented by temperature categories to understand consumer behavior:
* [cite_start]**Very Cold:** 53.16% of weekly sales [cite: 98]
* [cite_start]**Cold:** 36.23% of weekly sales [cite: 97]
* [cite_start]**Warm/Hot/Very Hot:** Combined these represent approximately 10.61% of sales[cite: 90].

The dashboard allows for granular data exploration through the following filters:
* [cite_start]**Store Selector:** Filter by individual store numbers[cite: 3, 4].
