# Walmart Sales Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview
This interactive dashboard provides a deep-dive analysis of **Walmart's sales data**, focusing on revenue trends, regional performance, and product category success. It is designed to help stakeholders visualize growth patterns and identify high-performing segments within the retail landscape.

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
* **Total Sales:** $120,166,650
* **Total Profit:** $332,134,761.45
* **Average Sales per Transaction:** Higher concentrations noted in specific regions like the West.

---

## Dashboard Visualizations & Sections

### 1. Sales Distribution by Method
A **Doughnut Chart** categorizes how customers are making purchases:
* **Online:** 37%
* **Outlet:** 33%
* **In-store:** 30%

### 2. Regional & Retailer Performance
* **Market Share by Retailer:** A pie chart shows the distribution of sales across major partners, with **West Gear** (26%) and **Foot Locker** (24%) leading the volume.
* **Sales Method by Retailer:** A stacked bar chart breaks down the fulfillment method for each retailer. Notably, **West Gear** shows a dominant volume in "In-store" sales compared to others.

### 3. Profitability by Product Category
The dashboard ranks product lines based on their total operating profit:
* **Top Performer:** Men's Street Footwear ($82.8M).
* **High Growth:** Women's Apparel ($68.6M) and Men's Athletic Footwear ($51.8M).
* **Emerging Categories:** Women's Street Footwear and Men's Apparel.

### 4. Time-Series Trend Analysis (2020 - 2021)
A dual-line graph compares the **Sum of Total Sales** against the **Sum of Operating Profit**:
* **Insight:** There is a significant upward trajectory starting in **Q1 2021**, with profit margins scaling faster than total sales volume toward the end of the year.

---

## Technical Features
* **Dynamic Slicers:** Allows users to filter the entire dashboard by **Retailer** (e.g., Amazon, Walmart, Kohl's) or **Product Category** (e.g., Men's vs. Women's gear).
* **Advanced Visuals:** Combines trend lines, doughnut charts, and stacked bar charts for a multi-dimensional view of the data.

---


## Key Business Insights
* **Digital Transformation:** With 37% of sales occurring online, digital channels are the primary revenue driver.
* **Seasonal Growth:** The sharp incline in 2021 suggests successful promotional periods or post-pandemic market recovery.
* **Strategic Partnerships:** West Gear is a critical partner, contributing over a quarter of the total sales volume analyzed.
